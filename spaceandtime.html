Space And Time

In code as in life, complexity is toxic and consequences are brutal. Time and space are precious and their wastage is unacceptable. On the above note, SITE in association with Invictus 2019 brings forth Space and Time complexity: a competition that will drive you to think beyond your limits.The participants will be given 4-6 problems and their brute force solutions. Their task will be to optimize the solution to the best of their abilities. The most optimized solution i.e. the one with minimum space and time complexity will be deemed the best. May the constraints be ever in your favour!


Date: 9 th February 2019
Time: 11 a.m. - 2 p.m.
Venue: SPS 5, DTU
