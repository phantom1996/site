GAME OF DOTS

If a daunting face-off enthralls you, winning board games has always been a piece of cake or overpowering the enemy is your natural instinct, we bring you a new digital arena for tackling yet another nerve wracking challenge. 'Game of Dots' has been curated by one of our very own DTUite to join the likes of Chess, Checkers and others where strategy, survival skills and tenacity are to be coupled with the most revered: Coding Ability, to guarantee a win. This interactive coding game will involve 2 players who will have to code their way ahead in the given software based game.


Date: 8th February, 2019
Time: 10 a.m. - 1 p.m.
Venue: CN and WEB Lab
